Welcome

This is our website for our CMSC 447 project. Our goal with this website is to encourage people to engage in environmental cleanup. We will have a map where once users login, they can pin locations that need cleanup, and also locations that have been recenty cleaned. On top of that, we will offer a fun game to futher encourage our users to care and recycle.

We have tabs that can assist the user if they need help, the lonk to our game and the map, and an about page wehre the user can ask for help or submit feedback.

Users can also use our login link to to access their account and utilize our map and game.
